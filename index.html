Can I Update My Order After Payment?

Absolutely! We understand plans can change, and we’re here to help. You can request design changes from the moment we start designing until we send the final design to you for approval. You can also adjust details like sheet size or type before we begin printing.
We begin designing within 24 hours of your order and start printing within 24 hours of design approval. If you need any updates, reach out quickly—we’re here to make your project perfect!

Do you offer design services?

Absolutely! We provide professional design services to bring your vision to life. Whether you need a brand-new design or tweaks to an existing one, our expert team is here to help. Share your ideas, and we’ll create something tailored just for you.

How Do I Place an Order?

Placing an order is easy:
Submit Your Design: Upload your design requirements or use our design tool to customize your wax paper.
Get a Quote: We’ll send you a tailored price and order details for approval.
Approve and Proceed: Once approved, we’ll start production and arrange delivery.
We ensure a smooth process and designs that match your needs!

Will I need to approve my artwork before it's printed?

You got it right! We’ll send you a proof of your artwork for approval before printing begins. This ensures everything meets your expectations and allows for any final adjustments. Your order won’t move to production until you’re completely satisfied!

Where can I buy Custom Wax Paper?

You can buy custom wax paper directly from us at WaxPaperGlobal! We specialize in creating high-quality, custom-printed wax paper, tailored specifically for food brands.
